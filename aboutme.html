About Me

My name is Peter Baah, and currently I work as Health Information Management Specialist.

I aspire to be a web developer and a social entrepreneur, I was always fascinated with the idea of how businessess can be used to solve problems and to provide opportunities for others.

I realized that, if businessess are ethically grounded it would do wonders for the world and is also key to lasting success. I joined the United States Army right after September 11, 2001.

As a purple heart veteran, I connect easily with veterans and anyone as well.
